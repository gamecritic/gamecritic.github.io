Those Who Remain Release Date Announced

10 May, 2020 by Alireza

Award-winning publisher Wired Productions and indie studio Camel 101 have announced the release date of the adventure thriller Those Who Remain. The game will be released on May 28, 2020 for Xbox One, PS4 and Windows PC via Steam. The Switch version, and a retail exclusive deluxe edition are planned for release in summer 2020.
